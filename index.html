After a few months of working with this group in creating a brand & as well as an effective advertisement for our baked cookies, I can tell that it was pleasant despite having a few difficulties such as misunderstanding and a lack of time management. Because the allocated member is knowledgeable in that field, editing was not a major issue on our part. Even though we went through a lot, we still finished it because of each other's cooperation.
